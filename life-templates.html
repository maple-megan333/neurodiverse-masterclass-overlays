Your Dopamine Menu

🎰
What this is
A tiered reward system for your brain. Instead of reaching for doom-scrolling when you need dopamine, pick from a menu of activities sorted by time and energy. No guilt, no shame — just honest labels.
🥗
Appetizers — 2-5 min
  • Watch one satisfying video
  • Text someone you appreciate
  • Step outside for sunlight
  • Play one song at full volume
  • Drink cold water mindfully
🍽
Sides — 10-20 min
  • Walk around the block
  • Quick creative sketch
  • Cook a simple snack
  • Reorganize one small space
  • Play a quick game
🍝
Entrees — 30-60 min
  • Work on a creative project
  • Exercise
  • Cook a new recipe
  • Call a friend
  • Learn something new
🍰
Desserts — 1+ hours
  • Full creative session
  • Long bath with candles/music
  • Proper movie night
  • Guilt-free hyperfocus dive
🍔
Junk Food — honest labels
  • Doom-scrolling
  • Unnecessary shopping
  • Unintentional binge-watching
  • Compulsive email refreshing
  • Starting new projects instead of finishing

Meal Planning

🍳
What this is
A meal planning system designed for ADHD brains. No complicated spreadsheets, no guilt. Plan 5 meals, let the rest flex. AI handles the shopping list.

The 5-Meal Formula

📘
Plan 5, not 7
  1. Monday: Easy comfort food
  2. Tuesday: New recipe try
  3. Wednesday: Leftovers or freezer meal
  4. Thursday: Batch cook
  5. Friday: Takeout / date night / cleanup

Saturday & Sunday: flex.
