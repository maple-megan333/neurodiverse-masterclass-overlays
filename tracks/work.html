Your AI Toolkit

AI at Work

Integrate AI into your professional workflow to boost productivity, clarity, and impact.

Email & Communications ▼

Master professional communication with AI assistance. Use Claude to help draft, refine, and perfect your emails, Slack messages, and other workplace communications.

Draft Emails at Scale: Share the context and tone, and get a draft to refine.

I need to send an email to [recipient] about [topic]. Here's what I want to convey: - [Key point 1] - [Key point 2] - [Key point 3] The tone should be [professional/casual/urgent]. Can you draft this for me?

Respond to Difficult Messages: Get help navigating tricky communications with diplomacy.

I received this message: "[paste message]" This is frustrating because [explain why]. I need to respond in a way that: - Addresses their concern - Sets a boundary about [specific thing] - Keeps the relationship intact Can you help me craft a response?

Summarize Long Threads: Use AI to extract key points from email chains.

Here's an email thread about [topic]. Can you: 1. Summarize the main points 2. Identify any open action items 3. Flag any conflicts or disagreements 4. Suggest next steps
Meeting Prep ▼

Prepare thoroughly for every meeting. Let AI help you research, strategize, and think through outcomes before you step into the room.

Pre-Meeting Strategy: Clarify your goals and prepare talking points.

I have a meeting with [person/team] about [topic] on [date]. Here's the context: [Background info] My goals for this meeting are: 1. [Goal 1] 2. [Goal 2] 3. [Goal 3] Can you help me prepare talking points and anticipate their questions?

Research Participants: Understand who'll be in the room and their perspective.

I'm meeting with [names]. Here's what I know about them: - [Person 1]: [Role/background] - [Person 2]: [Role/background] The topic is [topic]. What should I know about their likely perspective? What questions might they ask?

Meeting Agenda Optimization: Refine the agenda for better outcomes.

Here's the proposed agenda for our [duration] meeting: [Paste agenda] We need to cover [specific outcomes]. Does this agenda serve that? Any suggestions for making this meeting more effective?
Project Planning ▼

Turn vague project ideas into structured, actionable plans. Use AI to break down projects, identify risks, and create realistic timelines.

Break Down Complex Projects: Take a big goal and create a step-by-step plan.

I'm working on [project name]. Here's what we're trying to accomplish: [Project description] Timeline: [Start to end date] Team size: [Number and roles] Budget/resources: [Available resources] Can you break this into phases and create a realistic timeline? What dependencies should we watch for?

Risk Assessment: Identify what could go wrong before it does.

Here's our project plan: [Paste project outline] What are the biggest risks here? Where are we most likely to slip? What should we mitigate first?

Status Update Framework: Structure project communications clearly.

Project: [Name] Timeline: [Current phase] Key accomplishments this week: [List] Blockers: [List any] Next steps: [List] Can you format this into a clear status update for stakeholders?
Status Reports ▼

Write status reports that inform without overwhelming. Let AI help you translate raw work into clear, compelling updates.

Weekly Status Template: Structure your week into a compelling narrative.

This week I worked on: - [Task 1]: [Result/status] - [Task 2]: [Result/status] - [Task 3]: [Result/status] Key wins: [Highlight 1-2 accomplishments] Blockers: [Any challenges] Next week: [What's planned] Can you turn this into a polished status report? Make it punchy and highlight impact.

Translate Jargon to Impact: Make technical work meaningful to non-technical stakeholders.

Here's what our team accomplished: [Technical description of work] Audience: [Who's reading this - executives/clients/team] I need to explain why this matters to them. Can you translate this into business impact?

Problem Escalation Write-Up: Clearly communicate issues that need attention.

Issue: [What's going wrong] Impact: [Why it matters] Root cause: [Why it happened] Proposed solution: [How to fix] Timeline: [When we need this resolved] Can you write this up so leadership understands both urgency and what we're doing about it?
Spreadsheet Help ▼

Let AI assist with spreadsheet challenges, from formula creation to data analysis. Get help thinking through data problems logically.

Formula Troubleshooting: Debug formulas or create new ones.

I'm trying to [what you want to achieve] in my spreadsheet. My data looks like: [Describe structure: "Column A has names, Column B has dates, Column C has amounts"] I need a formula that [specific requirement]. I tried this but it's not working: [Paste your formula or describe what happened] Can you help me fix this?

Data Organization: Restructure messy data into usable format.

I have this data structure: [Paste or describe your current setup] I need it reorganized so that: [Describe what you're trying to see/analyze] Can you suggest the best way to restructure this? What columns/calculations do I need?

Analysis Strategy: Plan out complex analysis step-by-step.

Data: [Describe what you have] Question: [What are you trying to understand?] I need to analyze [specific aspect]. Walk me through the steps to answer this question with my data. What calculations should I do first?
