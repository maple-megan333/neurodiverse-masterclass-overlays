Quick Wins

Easy wins you can do today to start using AI better. No setup required.

5-10 min eachLow energyImmediate results
You don't need to be ready. Try these quick wins today and see what changes.

1. One-Sentence Custom Instructions

Takes 2 minutes

Go to Claude settings, add ONE sentence about how you work best. Example: "I have ADHD and prefer bullet points over paragraphs." Instantly improves every conversation.

2. Rubber Duck Debugging with AI

Takes 5 minutes

Stuck on a problem? Paste it to AI and say: "I'm going to explain this and I want you to just listen and reflect back what I say." Your brain figures it out as you explain.

3. The 30-Second Brainstorm

Takes 30 seconds

Say: "Give me 5 fast ideas for [task]." Don't overthink. React to the first thing that resonates. AI is a thinking partner, not a decision-maker.

4. Break Down Your Stuck Project

Takes 10 minutes

Paste your stalled project and ask: "Break this into 3 steps a ND person could do without getting overwhelmed." AI helps you see what's actually doable vs. what's perfectionism.

5. Voice-to-Text to AI

Takes 2 minutes setup

Dictate into your phone, paste the mess into AI, ask it to: "Clean this up, organize into sections, and make it concise." Your voice gets turned into writing.

💡
The point:

These aren't "proper" ways to use AI. They're messy, fast, and human. That's the whole idea. You're allowed to use AI like a thinking partner, not a professional tool.

💾Pick one quick win today. See what happens. You can learn the "right way" later.
