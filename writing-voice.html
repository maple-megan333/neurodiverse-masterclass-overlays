✂️ Editing as Thinking — 4 Editing Modes
📘
4 Modes
  • Clarity: Rewrite unclear sentences. Keep my voice.
  • Tone: Shift to be more [formal/casual/warm].
  • Argument: Read as a skeptic. Find weakest points.
  • Brevity: Cut by 30%. Show what you removed.
