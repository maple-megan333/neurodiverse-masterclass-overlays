Tips for Best Results

✨How to use these prompts
  • Copy the whole thing. Don't retype. Copy-paste is your friend.
  • Fill in the brackets. [like this] means you put your own info there.
  • Ask a follow-up. If Claude's first answer isn't quite right, ask them to adjust. "Make it shorter" or "Make it more funny" works great.
  • Save what works. If you use a prompt and love it, copy it to a notes file so you have it next time.
