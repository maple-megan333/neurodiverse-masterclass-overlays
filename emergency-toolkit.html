Emergency Toolkit

Crisis prompts for when your brain is offline.

When to use this
When you can't think clearly, can't decide, can't start, or can't stop spiraling. These work even when your brain doesn't.
☁️
How to use
Find your state below. Copy the prompt. Paste into Claude. Follow what it says.
🧊 Can't Start 🌊 Overwhelmed ⚖️ Can't Decide 🔥 Burned Out 😔 Shame

🧊 Task Paralysis

You know what to do but can't start.

✏️
Copy and paste
I'm completely stuck. Here's what's on my plate: [LIST]

Don't give me a plan. Just:
1. The ONE smallest/easiest thing to do first
2. The exact first physical action
3. A timer: how many minutes before I can stop guilt-free

🌊 Emotional Flooding

Everything feels like too much.

✏️
Copy and paste
I'm emotionally overwhelmed. I don't need solutions.

Talk to me like a calm, kind friend. Help me:
1. Name what I might be feeling (give options)
2. Do one grounding thing (simple, physical)
3. Remind me this is temporary

Short response. I can't read a lot right now.

⚖️ Decision Paralysis

Every option feels wrong. Stuck in a loop.

✏️
Copy and paste
I can't make this decision: [DESCRIBE]

Just pick one for me. I give you permission. Tell me:
1. Your pick
2. One reason
3. What to do in the next 5 minutes

No pros and cons. Just pick.

🔥 Burnout Recovery

Running on empty. Everything feels pointless.

✏️
Copy and paste
I'm burned out. Help me with TODAY only:
1. Absolute minimum I must do? (truly urgent only)
2. What can I skip?
3. One tiny enjoyable thing (not productive — genuinely fun)

Be gentle. I'm running on empty.

😔 Shame Spiral

Inner critic is loud. Feel like you've messed up.

✏️
Copy and paste
I'm in a shame spiral about [WHAT HAPPENED].

I don't need "it's okay" — my brain won't believe it. Instead:
1. Separate facts from feelings
2. What would I say to a friend in this situation?
3. What's the smallest repair action?

Be honest but kind.

Save this page
Bookmark it. Save to your phone's home screen. When crisis hits, you won't remember it exists — make it impossible to forget.

After the Storm

When the crisis passes and you can think again.

💜
Gentle self-check
When you're ready (no rush), ask Claude: 
I just came out of [task paralysis / emotional flooding / decision paralysis / burnout / shame spiral]. Help me do a gentle 2-minute self-check:
1. What helped me get through it?
2. What made it worse?
3. One small thing I can do differently next time.
Keep it compassionate. No lectures.
