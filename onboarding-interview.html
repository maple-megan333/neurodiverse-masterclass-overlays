🧠 Phase 1: How Do You Learn Best?

✏️
Do this now
I'm setting up my AI tools and want to understand how my brain works best. Ask me 5 quick questions (one at a time) about:
- Whether I learn better by reading, listening, watching, or doing
- What time of day my brain works best
- How long I can focus before needing a break
- Whether I prefer details-first or big-picture-first
- What happens when I'm overwhelmed

After my answers, give me a 3-4 sentence summary labeled "Learning Style & Energy Patterns." Keep it conversational.
