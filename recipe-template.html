The 5-Meal Formula

📘
Plan 5, not 7
  1. Monday: Easy comfort food
  2. Tuesday: New recipe try
  3. Wednesday: Leftovers or freezer meal
  4. Thursday: Batch cook
  5. Friday: Takeout / date night / cleanup

Saturday & Sunday: flex.
